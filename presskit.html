Craggenrock is a crafting shop management game taking place in a remote island after a great disaster. The great evil has been defeated but monsters are still running amok. As a crafter, take this opportunity to improve your skills and make some money.

The Developer

Ardeimon

Rexter Day Tolentino is Ardeimon. He is an aspiring game developer based in Cavite, PH. He has mainly worked on game jams and produced games like Gastronom, Delve and Pip and Waddleton. He graduated from University of the Philippines Visayas, with a Bachelor of Arts (Psychology) degree in 2017.
