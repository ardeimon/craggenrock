Explore life in Pareho with hammer (and ledger) in tow

Opportunity strikes!

A young, aspiring blacksmith named Arlo returns to the island where he spent his apprenticeship after a recent worldwide disaster. With monsters and new strange land formations aplenty, adventurers flocked to the island in search of riches and fame.

Join Arlo on his arrival to the island to establish his name and discover what these developments are as he opens his new shop in Craggenrock.

The daily grind

Establishing a shop in Pareho is busy work.
Procure materials from merchants

Get to know the local merchants in town. Enter the woodcutter's grocer shop. Order ores and ingots in advance from the mining office. Bargain with the merchants in the market for herbs and monster parts.

Craft Items in your workshop

The forge and anvil are hot and ready for your metals. Whittle wood with the workbench. Spin and weave fibers and cloths with the spinning wheel. Make that leather with the tanning rack. And who could forget your trusty crafting bench?

Sell items in your shop

Set prices for all similar items in display or mark a special price for one shelf.
Man that counter and fill that register with your customer's hard-earned money.
If they haggle, just make sure you'll get the most out of it!

  • 200+ materials to experiment with to craft your products
  • Over 700 recipes available to explore for maximum profit
  • Accept requests to craft, repair or upgrade your customer's gear.

Some other things to look out for

  • Shop for seasonal materials as months go by.
  • Care for the environment by recycling junk materials.
  • Issue orders to adventurers to get the items you need.
  • Watch out for random events that affect the town, the merchants and... your sales!
  • Upgrade your sales license to get more, better-paying (but more meticulous) customers.
